Vocab Review Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Vocab Review is a private beta vocabulary review app. This policy explains what data the web app, Chrome extension, and iOS app use to provide capture, library, review, and reminder features.

Data We Collect

  • Email address used for magic-link sign-in.
  • Vocabulary cards you create, including terms, meanings, examples, notes, and review history.
  • Source text, page titles, and source URLs captured from the Chrome extension or iOS share extension.
  • Session tokens used to keep you signed in.
  • Local notification reminder data generated by the iOS app for upcoming reviews.

How We Use Data

We use this data to save vocabulary, schedule spaced-repetition reviews, sync your library across clients, support beta debugging, and show local reminders on iOS when you request them.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is not shared with advertisers. The beta backend may use service providers for hosting, database storage, and optional vocabulary auto-completion.

Storage and Deletion

Your data is stored in the Vocab Review production backend. During the private beta, account or data deletion is handled by request.

Contact

For beta support or deletion requests, contact the Vocab Review maintainer who invited you to the beta.
